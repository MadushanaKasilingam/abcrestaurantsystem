Our Story: A Taste of Sri Lanka

ABC Restaurant began with a simple idea—bringing people together through food. As a multi-cuisine restaurant with branches across Sri Lanka, we offer a blend of traditional Sri Lankan flavors and international dishes. With each meal, we strive to deliver quality, freshness, and innovation, making every dining experience memorable. From our first location to many across the island, ABC Restaurant continues to celebrate the diversity and richness of cuisine, offering something for everyone.
